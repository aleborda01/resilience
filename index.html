FAQ

Do I need an idea to apply?

Not at all. Many cohort members are simply talented, curious, and ambitious builders. If you do have an idea, you'll make it stronger during the program.

What's the time commitment?

3-4 hours per week for sessions and office hours. You can participate while doing your PhD, postdoc, or full-time job. The program runs for 14 weeks.

Can I participate while working at a lab?

Yes. Sessions are held evenings and weekends, with remote options for participants outside San Francisco. What matters is showing up and pursuing your ideas with founder intensity.

Is this only for for-profit startups?

No. AI resilience often requires non-market incentives. We support nonprofit and hybrid structures, and help teams choose the structure that best supports their mission.

What happens after the program?

You join a lifelong community of AI resilience founders. Alumni stay connected, refer talent to each other, and often collaborate on new projects.

Is this really free?

Yes. No equity, no fees, no weird clauses. The goal is to create more world-changing organizations working on AI resilience.
